**THE SITUATION**  
*Jumpstart Gas Station – Wellington, KS*  

- **Location:** 112 N Main St, Wellington (pop. ≈ 8,200).  
- **Traffic:** KS‑7 sees ~5,500 veh/day; 30 % (≈ 1,650) pass within 0.25 mi of the station.  
- **Fuel sales (estimate):** 2,000 gal/day @ $2.85/gal → **$5,700 daily** → **$2.08 M /yr**.  
- **Convenience‑store sales (estimate):** $1,200 daily (average basket $4, 300 items) → **$438 k /yr**.  
- **Current gross margins:**  
  - Fuel: $0.05 / gal → **$100 / day** (≈ $36.5 k /yr).  
  - Store: $0.30 / item → **$90 / day** (≈ $32.9 k /yr).  
- **Labor:** Owner “doesn’t pay employees.” In reality the station runs 2 attendants (≈ 80 h / wk). Legal payroll ≈ $12 / hr → **$1,920 / wk** → **$100 k /yr** (plus $15 k /yr payroll taxes).  
- **Compliance risk:** No payroll → **$10 k – $30 k** in back‑pay penalties per employee, plus potential OSHA/health‑code fines.  

**Bottom line:** The station is *generating* roughly **$69 k /yr** gross profit **while sitting on a $115 k /yr legal liability** and losing sales because shelves are empty.
